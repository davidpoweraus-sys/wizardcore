üí° Color Usage Guidelines

‚úì DO

‚Ä¢ Use navy for text on light backgrounds
‚Ä¢ Leverage metallic golds for premium features
‚úó DON'T
‚Ä¢ Overuse bright coral - it loses impact
‚Ä¢ Use navy text on dark backgrounds
‚Ä¢ Mix too many metallics in one view
‚Ä¢ Ignore contrast ratios for accessibility
‚úì DO
‚Ä¢ Use gradients for backgrounds and headers
‚Ä¢ Add texture overlays for Art Deco feel
‚Ä¢ Use cream (#f5e6d3) as primary background
‚Ä¢ Apply consistent border styles (3px solid)
‚úó DON'T

‚Ä¢ Mix competing gradients
‚Ä¢ Use pure white everywhere - cream is warmer
‚Ä¢ Make borders too thin (<2px)
